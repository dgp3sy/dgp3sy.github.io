About the Artist

I am 23 years old, and I am self-taught through readings, analysis of other artists, and online resources. I have been painting as a personal hobby since March 2020. I paint in primarily (14" x 14"), (8" x 10"), (9" x 12"), and (12" x 16"). I hope to continually advance to larger scale works as my skills develop. I work with acrylic paints applied on white canvas. My subject matter includes still life paintings, where I focus on the technical aspects of composition, light, and form. I also enjoy painting landscapes, especially scenes from America's National Parks, which allows me to focus on perspective, color mixing, and composition. I find inspiration from Winslow Homer, James Whistler, and Thomas Moran.
