About Me

I am currently pursing a Bachelor's Degree at the University of Virginia, with a double major in Computer Science and Economics. I am interested in a career in Software Development and I have enjoyed working on projects through school, work, and personal interests. I have experience with projects involving Java, Python, C++, and Web Development. I have also enjoyed projects in Data Analysis including building a Neural Network, Character Recognition, and Machine Learning.

In addition, I have applied my skills to provide consulting services for Dominion Energy and the Culinary Institute. Through these projects, I have provided Data Analytics and Competitive Analysis of each company's role within their respective industry. In my free time, I serve as the Online Services Chair for Virginia Ambassadors, the Office of Admission's student volunteer group and provide academic advising to younger students. In five years, I hope to be a professional in Software Engineering in a challenging and engaging setting.
